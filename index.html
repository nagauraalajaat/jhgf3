This website provides essential information and step-by-step guides for students, parents, and educators related to the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE), Ajmer. Whether you're looking to check your results, download the latest syllabus, or understand the re-evaluation process, you'll find helpful resources here.

The Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE) is a board of education for school-level education in the Indian state of Rajasthan. RBSE is responsible for the promotion and development of secondary education in Rajasthan.

How-To Guides

How to Check RBSE Results

Checking your RBSE 10th or 12th class results is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to access your scores quickly:

  1. Visit the official RBSE results portal (link provided on the official website).
  2. Look for the link related to your class (e.g., "Secondary Result 2024" or "Senior Secondary Result 2024").
  3. Enter your Roll Number in the designated field.
  4. Click on the "Submit" or "Get Result" button.
  5. Your result will be displayed on the screen. It's advisable to take a printout for future reference.

How to Download RBSE Syllabus

Staying updated with the latest syllabus is crucial for exam preparation. Here's how you can download it:

  1. Go to the official RBSE website.
  2. Navigate to the "Academics" or "Syllabus" section.
  3. Select your class (e.g., Class 10, Class 12) and the academic year.
  4. Click on the subject-wise links to download the PDF files of the syllabus.
  5. Save the PDFs to your device for offline access.

How to Apply for Re-evaluation / Scrutiny

If you are not satisfied with your marks, you can apply for re-evaluation or scrutiny. Here's the general procedure:

  1. Check the official RBSE website for the re-evaluation application dates and guidelines.
  2. Fill out the online application form with your details and the subjects you wish to re-evaluate.
  3. Pay the required application fee online.
  4. Submit the application. Keep a copy of the acknowledgment for your records.
  5. Results for re-evaluation will be announced on the official portal.

How to Get Duplicate Mark Sheet / Certificate

Lost your mark sheet or certificate? Here's how you can apply for a duplicate copy:

  1. Download the application form for duplicate documents from the RBSE website.
  2. Fill in all the required details accurately.
  3. Attach necessary documents like an affidavit (if required) and proof of identity.
  4. Pay the prescribed fee via Demand Draft or online as specified.
  5. Submit the application form along with documents and fee receipt to the RBSE office, or send via registered post.

How to Check Exam Timetable

The RBSE releases the examination timetable well in advance. Here's how to find it:

  1. Visit the "News Update" or "Examination" section on the official RBSE website.
  2. Look for the link titled "Time Table" or "Exam Schedule" for your respective class (e.g., 10th or 12th).
  3. Click on the link to open the PDF file of the timetable.
  4. Download and save the timetable for future reference.

How to Download Admit Card

Admit cards are mandatory for appearing in exams. Here's how to download yours:

  1. Visit the official RBSE website's admit card portal (usually linked from the homepage during exam season).
  2. Enter your credentials, such as your application number or roll number, and date of birth.
  3. Click on "Download Admit Card" or "Get Hall Ticket."
  4. Your admit card will be displayed. Verify all details and take a printout.
